# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible
disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual
identity and orientation.

## Our standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy and
kindness, being respectful of differing opinions, giving and gracefully accepting constructive
feedback, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct which could
reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at
nicolas.schmid.research@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly.
